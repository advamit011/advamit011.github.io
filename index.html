Frequently Asked Questions
What types of cases do you handle? +
I handle Criminal, Cyber, Civil, NDPS, Enforcement Directorate (PMLA/FEMA), Family Law, and Intellectual Property Rights matters at District, Sessions, High Court and Tribunal levels.
How do I book a consultation? +
You can reach out via the contact form on this website, call directly at 9650471903, or send an enquiry. An initial consultation will be scheduled at your convenience.
Do you handle cases outside Delhi? +
Yes, I practice before various High Courts and Tribunals. For matters in other states, I coordinate with local advocates as required for effective representation.
What is your approach to Cyber Law cases? +
With both a BSc in Computer Science and an LLM in Cyber Law, I bring a unique technical-legal perspective to cybercrime cases, digital evidence analysis, and online fraud matters under the IT Act 2000.
Can you assist with ED / PMLA investigations? +
Absolutely. I provide comprehensive advisory and representation in Enforcement Directorate matters including PMLA, FEMA, asset attachment challenges, and money laundering defense strategies.
Do you offer legal opinions and documentation? +
Yes, beyond courtroom representation I also provide legal opinions, contract drafting and review, compliance advisory, and documentation services for individuals and businesses.
